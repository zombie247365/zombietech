How ZombieTech works for site owners
1
List your site in 20 minutes
Upload your lease and bank statements. Our AI calculates a fair hourly rate from your actual costs — not a guess. You set your available zombie hours and choose your upside model.
2
We find you a vetted operator
Zombie operators browse your listing and send a booking request with their trading concept. You review and approve — 48-hour window. Once matched, a standard zombie contract is generated and both parties sign digitally.
3
The night runs on rails
The operator uses our app to photograph your kitchen before and after every session. Digital handover at 20:00. Timestamped photo evidence of lock-up. You don't need to be there.
4
Review and get paid on Monday
Sunday morning you receive an AI-scored handover report comparing before and after photos across every surface. Confirm good order or raise a claim by Monday noon. Settlement processes Monday afternoon.
What you earn
Your earnings are calculated from your own lease and utility costs. Choose a guaranteed fixed upside (e.g. 30% above cost recovery) or a variable share of your zombie operator's net profit. Both options are explained and modelled before you sign anything.

This is the standard ZombieTech services agreement — specific to your site and your chosen commercials. Read each section before signing.
Commercials
Site fee: R29.86/hr (cost recovery) + 30% fixed upside = R38.82/hr guaranteed payable to site owner per contracted zombie hour
Settlement: Site owner confirms site condition by Monday 12:00. ZombieTech settles all payments Monday afternoon.
ZombieTech fee: 10% of zombie operator gross revenue — not deducted from site owner income
Obligations — site owner
Site owner retains possession at all times and may access the premises during zombie hours. Operator's right of use is a limited, revocable license — not a sublease or transfer of possession.
Site owner must ensure all listed equipment is in working order at each handover. Failures that prevent the operator from trading trigger a proportional fee reduction.
Termination & goodwill
30-day notice: required from either party without cause.
Site owner termination fee: 15% of final settlement + goodwill fee if operator has completed 6+ clean sessions.
Operator breach: immediate termination, penalties apply as defined in Schedule A.
